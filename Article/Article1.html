BMW 8 Series: A Comprehensive Review

Luxury, performance, and versatility in one remarkable lineup.

The BMW 8 Series combines luxury and performance across models like the 840i, 850i, and M8 trims. Available as a coupe, convertible, or gran coupe, it offers style, power, and versatility.

Introduction to the BMW 8 Series

The BMW 8 Series is a pinnacle of luxury and performance in BMW's lineup, blending cutting-edge technology with powerful drivetrains and striking designs. Available in three distinct body styles—coupe, convertible, and gran coupe—the 8 Series offers versatility to suit diverse driving preferences. Within the lineup, the 840i, 850i, M8, and M8 Competition trims each cater to a unique blend of comfort, performance, and exclusivity.

Model and Trim Lineup

BMW 840i

The 840i represents the gateway to the 8 Series. Its turbocharged inline-6 engine provides a refined balance of power and efficiency, ideal for everyday luxury driving.

  • Engine: 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6
  • Power: 335 hp / 368 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 4.7 seconds (xDrive)
  • Price: ~$87,500

BMW 850i xDrive

Stepping up to the 850i introduces a significant power increase with its turbocharged V8. Acceleration is thrilling, and the xDrive system ensures excellent grip in all conditions.

  • Engine: 4.4L TwinPower Turbo V8
  • Power: 523 hp / 553 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 3.8 seconds
  • Price: ~$100,400

BMW M8

The M8 elevates the 8 Series to supercar territory with a specially tuned V8 that offers staggering performance. It features enhanced suspension and braking systems for precise handling.

  • Engine: 4.4L TwinPower Turbo V8
  • Power: 600 hp / 553 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 3.1 seconds
  • Price: ~$135,000

BMW M8 Competition

For the ultimate in performance, the M8 Competition pushes boundaries with additional horsepower and sharper suspension settings. It’s the epitome of BMW engineering.

  • Engine: 4.4L TwinPower Turbo V8
  • Power: 617 hp / 553 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 3.0 seconds
  • Price: ~$146,000

Body Style Options

Coupe

The coupe features a sleek roofline and bold proportions, ideal for drivers seeking the sportiest look and feel.

Convertible

Open-air driving at its best. The convertible comes with a retractable soft top, offering a dynamic and immersive driving experience.

Gran Coupe

The gran coupe blends practicality with style, featuring four doors, a larger trunk, and added rear-seat comfort.

Interior and Technology

The 8 Series interior is a perfect blend of luxury and innovation, featuring BMW’s latest iDrive 7.0 system, customizable digital instrument clusters, and premium audio options. Higher trims add unique styling and exclusive features such as carbon-fiber accents and M-specific seating.

Conclusion

The BMW 8 Series redefines luxury and performance. From the refined 840i to the high-performance M8 Competition, this lineup offers something for everyone. Whether you choose the coupe, convertible, or gran coupe, you’re guaranteed a driving experience that’s second to none.
